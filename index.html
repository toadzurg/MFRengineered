Meet Miranda

Miranda Fae Robinson is the founder and principal engineer of MFR Engineering, PLLC, a forward-thinking firm specializing in water resource solutions, renewable energy sites and sustainable engineering. Miranda is a licensed Professional Civil Engineer in New York and numerous other states, bringing a wealth of expertise and a broad perspective to every project.

As a proud MWBE-certified (Minority and Women-Owned Business Enterprise) company in New York State, MFR Engineering, PLLC is committed to delivering exceptional quality and innovative results while promoting diversity and inclusion in the industry.

With years of experience and a passion for excellence, Miranda leads every project with integrity, technical expertise, and a dedication to building a better, more sustainable future for our communities.
